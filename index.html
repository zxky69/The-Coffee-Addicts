Our History

Brewed with passion, our journey began with a simple love for great coffee. Founded in 2012 in a cozy corner of our hometown, we started as a small café with a big dream—to serve handcrafted coffee made from ethically sourced beans. Over the years, our dedication to quality, sustainability, and community has transformed us from a neighborhood favorite into a growing family of coffee lovers. From bean to cup, every step reflects our commitment to rich flavor, honest sourcing, and warm connections. Today, we continue to brew that same passion into every sip, sharing our love for coffee with the world—one cup at a time.
